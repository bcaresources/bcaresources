About Me

My name is Divyansh, and I developed this website as part of my final year project for the Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) program. The goal of this platform is to make it easier for BCA students to access, share, and manage academic resources in one place. This project reflects my interest in web development and my desire to build something genuinely useful for fellow students. I hope this website continues to support learners beyond my academic journey.
